Fil-Am singer-songwriter and 11th season runner up of the famed “American idol”, Jessica Sanchez is back in Cebu City! Jessica had her first major concert in Cebu in 2012 and she’s back for another one tonight titled “In the Spotlight Jessica Sanchez”. The show will be held at 8 PM (Nov. 29) at the Cebu Waterfront Hotel & Casino. The concert is directed by Paolo Valenciano with guest performers Lance Busa and Lucas Garcia.
